About Us

Student Trade Shop is an intercollege platform based in MITAOE, Pune, Maharashtra that facilitates the buying and selling of second-hand electronics and furniture exclusively for students. Our aim is to provide a sustainable and affordable solution for students who are looking for quality products without breaking the bank. We understand the financial constraints that come with being a student, which is why we have created a platform that allows students to buy and sell items at a fraction of the cost. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy for students to find what they need and connect with other students in the area. Join us today and start swapping!
